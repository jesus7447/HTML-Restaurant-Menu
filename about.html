Origin Story

Welcome to H-Thai-ML! Our story begins in 1986 in the city of Lampang, Thailand with a young boy named Ananda(means happy). After the passing of his father he worked as a fisher man to provide for his mother and four siblings. Although life was rough he never let that deter his ambitions of living a happy life as his name suggests. When he wasn't working, he was helping his mother prepare meals for his family. His mother was said to be the best cook in thier village. She passed on those special skills to him. He came to the realization that there was more to life than was suggested by others in his village. He arrived in the United States along with his family. To make endsmeat he worked at a local Thai restaurant. One day he was tasked with deiliverning food to Long Beach State Universtiy. He came across a programming class and was mesmorized. He decided to take a class and became a great progammer. Programming changed the life of his family and himself. Thus he opened his first Thai restaurant in Long Beach where his programming story began. The name honors the very thing that helped shape this new life he built. While also sharing the amazing gift of his mother's wonderful food. So come on in! And yes, our name is awesome. But our food is even better.
